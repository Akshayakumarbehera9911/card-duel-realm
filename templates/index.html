How to Play

1. Each player starts with a deck of 50 cards.

2. Players draw one card each round.

3. Players take turns choosing which attribute to compare (HP, Attack, Defense, Speed, or Intelligence).

4. The player with the higher value wins the round and gains a point.

5. Play continues for 50 rounds, and the player with more points at the end wins.
